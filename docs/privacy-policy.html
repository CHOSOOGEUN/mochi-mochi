Privacy Policy

App: Mochi Mochi
Developer: PuffStudio
Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PuffStudio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Mochi Mochi mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The App does not require account registration or login.

2. Data Stored Locally

The App saves the following data locally on your device only using AsyncStorage:

  • Game progress (high score, coins, level)
  • Purchased skins and items
  • Language preference
  • Daily bonus claim date

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple or Google.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
